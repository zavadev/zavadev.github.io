About Me

After many years of working in the film industry, I am excited to bring my skillset to a new dream and a new passion -- software engineering. Like many of my favorite things in life, I found my path to coding as an unexpected blessing. It didn't take long before the process of picking up new languages, creative problem solving, and the prospect of a career of constant learning had me hooked.

I have been delighted to find that the skills and experience I bring from my screenwriting and film background have a tremendous overlap with software engineering. I bring with me a growth-mindset focused on constant improvement, experience with collaboration on living documents with an iterative, version-controlled approach, and excellent written communication skills.

When I'm not at the computer, you'll find me running trails, training in various martial arts, meditating, or intently catching up on Ozark and all the other shows I missed while in my coding rabbit-holes.

Projects

DojoDeals

DojoDeals is a martial arts supply storefront designed with a modern, clean visual style in mind. User authorization is fully implemented, allowing authorized users the ability to interact with listed products, adding them to their Shopping Cart, and updating or deleting quantities as they wish, and of course a Checkout feature that will clear their cart. Full CRUD is also available on Product Reviews, allowing users to create and share their reviews on all listed items in the store.

Built with JavaScript, React, Redux, Python, Flask, SQLAlchemy, PostgreSQL, Heroku, HTML5, CSS3

Trapper Keeper

Trapper Keeper is a note-taking app inspired by the 80s and 90s binders of the same name. The app has full CRUD for Notebooks and Notes features as well as full user authentication and an emphasis on a bold design developed with vanilla CSS3.

Built with JavaScript, React, Redux, Node.js, Express, Sequelize, PostgreSQL, Heroku, HTML5, CSS3

Gora

Gora is a Question-and-Answer app inspired by Quora with a Horror genre theme. Users can come to seek answers to questions relating to Horror films, literature, and video games. The app is complete with full CRUD for Questions, Answers, multi-threaded Comments, and also sports User Profiles, Categories, and a Search bar for narrowing results to relevant posts.

Built with JavaScript, Pug, Node.js, Express, Sequelize, PostgreSQL, Heroku, HTML5, CSS3

BossShots

BossShots is an image-sharing app for showing off your epic boss battles from your favorite video games. The app features full CRUD for Photos, Comments, Photo Albums, and Photo Tags, as well as User Authentication. Images uploaded by the user are conducted with AWS S3 integration.

Built with JavaScript, React, Redux, Python, Flask, SQLAlchemy, PostgreSQL, Heroku, HTML5, CSS3
